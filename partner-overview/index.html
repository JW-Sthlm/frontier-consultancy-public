The Partner Challenge

Delivery is changing faster than practices.

  • Expert consultant time consumed by low-value tasks AI can handle today.
  • Skill shortage across Azure, D365, and Security delivery practices.
  • Customer expectations on cost, speed, and AI-readiness rising quarter on quarter.
  • AI-first delivery is becoming the new baseline. Partners who build the muscle now keep margin when it does.
  • Training alone does not change practice. Doing the work does.
The Engagement

An AI-first motion you learn by doing.

Frontier Consultancy equips a partner team to deliver AI-first work end to end. The mechanism is customer-zero: your team builds the playbook and the toolstack by running a real internal project before any paying customer is on the hook.

Build AND operate. The same agent fleet that builds the platform stays attached to run it. Project revenue on the build, recurring revenue on the operating layer. One toolstack does both.

  • Hands-on workshop using your own laptops, tools, and repos.
  • Real agents, MCP servers, and Copilot patterns built during the engagement.
  • Customer-zero project run by your team, coached by Microsoft.
  • Reusable artifacts you keep and redeploy on the next paying engagement.
