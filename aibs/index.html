AI Business Solutions · AIBS

Build AI-first delivery on Dynamics 365.

A 60 to 90 minute partner workshop for Dynamics 365 delivery teams. Walk through the lifecycle of an AI-first project, see the toolbelt your consultants get, and leave with the material to run it inside your organization.

What you get

A working delivery motion, not a webinar.

CRM and ERP are being reshaped by MCP servers, Copilot Studio, and AI-first configuration patterns. Dynamics 365 partners who build this motion now have a real window to differentiate — faster delivery, smaller teams, better outcomes.

This workshop walks you through the lifecycle of an AI-first Dynamics project: client brief, planning, demo, configuration, go-live prep. You see the actual toolbelt the field is using on Dynamics 365: CRM and ERP MCP servers, Copilot Studio agents, vibe configuration, and the Zava warehouse scenario that ties it together.

The deck is the source of truth. Supporting kit — exercise scenario, setup tiers, FAQ — follows in the next iteration.

Setup tiers · coming with the exercise kit

Three depths. Pick the one your room needs.

The deck lands regardless of setup. Hands-on depth changes based on what your consultants have ready.

Tier 1 · Watch and discuss
No setup

Run through the deck and the scenario as a discussion. No installs. Best when you want a leadership-level conversation.

Tier 2 · Copilot CLI
10 min setup

Each consultant has GitHub Copilot CLI. Walk the exercise live with Dynamics MCP servers, no deployment needed.

Tier 3 · Full agent stack
30+ min setup

Copilot CLI plus Dynamics MCP servers plus Copilot Studio. Build, configure, and demonstrate inside the session.
