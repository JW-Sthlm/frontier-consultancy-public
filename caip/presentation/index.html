BONUS DISCUSSION · OPT-IN

What prompt would you run with your customer tomorrow?

Pick one real customer. Pick one real outcome. Write the prompt you would actually paste into Copilot CLI on Monday morning, before any kick-off or proposal.

  • 190 seconds solo. No laptops, no talking. Pen and paper or a sticky note.
  • 22 minutes pair share. Swap with the person next to you. One question only: 'is this specific enough?'
  • 33 minutes share-back. Three or four people read theirs out loud. No discussion, just hearing them.

Use mid-exercise if energy needs a lift, or as a closer if you have 5–7 minutes spare. Capture the prompts in chat. They become each partner's first artefact from the day.
